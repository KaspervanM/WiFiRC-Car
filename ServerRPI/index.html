Settings

Drive mode

In tank mode, both motor are at the back and by changing the speeds, you can get some steering.

In car mode, one motor is at the back, serving as a thrust motor, and one is up front, for the steering.

Fine tuning

This (virtually) reverses the polarity of the left motor. This can be useful if you plugged the motor wrongly.

This (virtually) reverses the polarity of the right motor. This can be useful if you plugged the motor wrongly.

This (virtually) makes the left motor right and vice versa. This can be useful if you reversed the motors.
